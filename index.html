THE CROSS-SUBSIDY

THE ECONOMIC ENGINE

How does a public-access R&D lab survive without charging innovators agency-level fees? By employing a Robin Hood economic model: corporate operations subsidize public democratization.

Corporate Subsidy (B2B)

The physical infrastructure and tooling overhead of Idea Laboratory are maintained entirely by our high-margin, professional engineering channels.

  • High-Tier Consultancy: Providing rapid prototyping, defense tech, and hardware R&D services for established corporate clients.
  • CSR Funding Allocation: Partnering with massive tech corporations to legally route their mandatory Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds directly into backing independent innovators.
  • Government Grants: Leveraging existing hardware infrastructure grants.

The Public Royalty Cycle

Independent innovators access the Lab's R&D capabilities for a fraction of traditional costs, paying the Lab back only when they succeed.

  • Capped Royalties: We take a minor, strictly capped royalty or equity stake in the hardware startups we co-develop.
  • Ecosystem Autonomy: When an innovator secures massive seed funding or reaches market, their royalty returns to the Lab, funding the prototype cycle for the next common-man innovator.
